
# Adecco HR Analytics – Employee Attrition Analysis (Excel)

An end-to-end **HR Analytics** project developed in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and performance. The project uses Excel's analytical capabilities to identify key drivers of attrition and presents the findings through interactive dashboards.

---

## Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the most critical challenges faced by organizations. This project analyzes employee data to uncover trends, identify high-risk employee groups, and provide actionable insights that can help HR teams improve employee retention and workforce planning.

---

## Business Objectives

- Calculate the overall employee attrition rate.
- Identify departments and job roles with high attrition.
- Analyze employee demographics and workforce distribution.
- Understand factors influencing employee attrition.
- Evaluate employee satisfaction and performance.
- Build interactive Excel dashboards for HR decision-making.
- Provide data-driven business recommendations.

## Key Insights

- Overall employee attrition rate is **16.12%**.
- Single employees experience significantly higher attrition than married and divorced employees.
- Employees without stock options have higher attrition.
- Employees receiving no training have the highest attrition.
- Overtime is associated with increased employee attrition.
- Employee age and tenure show minimal relationship with job satisfaction.
- Business travel has little impact on employee satisfaction.
- Work-life balance has minimal effect on performance ratings.
